Overview

Bender Bending Rodriguez, commonly known as Bender, is a fictional character in the animated television series Futurama. He is a bending unit robot and one of the main characters in the show.

Bender is known for his sarcastic and cynical personality. Despite his flaws, he often provides comic relief and is a beloved character among fans of the series.

Throughout the series, Bender embarks on various adventures with his friends from Planet Express, including Fry, Leela, and the rest of the crew. His mischievous and often self-serving nature leads to both humorous and heartwarming moments.

Timeline of Bender's life:

  • 2996 - Bender is built.
  • 3000 - Bender meets Fry and joins Planet Expres.
  • 3008 - Bender becomes a professional wrestler.
  • 3012 - Bender runs for the office of the League of Robots.
  • 3014 - Bender creates a clone named Ben.
  • 3016 - Bender goes on a crime spree and gets imprisoned.
  • 3017 - Bender is released from prison and changes his name to Coilette.
  • 3020 - Bender cheats in a miniature golf tournament.
  • 3028 - Bender becomes a paparazzo.
  • 3030 - Bender stars in the TV show 'All My Circuits'.
  • 3031 - Bender forms the robot religion 'Benderism'.
  • 3037 - Bender becomes human but returns to his robot body.
  • 3041 - Bender continues his adventures with friends.
